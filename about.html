Behind the Brew

Discover our uncompromising obsession with agricultural traceability, roasting physics, and human connection.

The Seed to Cup Philosophy

Project Nexus began with a straightforward question: How can we elevate the brilliant coffee harvested across East Africa and serve it with absolute sensory transparency? We don't buy bulk commodity beans. Instead, we establish ethical, direct-trade partnerships with local smallholder co-operatives in Nyeri, Kericho, and the slopes of Mount Elgon.

By bypassing unnecessary supply-chain intermediaries, we ensure that farm owners are paid a premium wage that sits far above standard Fairtrade minimum values. This direct investment empowers our farming partners to maintain strict quality control and practice regenerative agronomy, ensuring sustainable agricultural land for generations to come.

Our Quality Pillars

  • ★
    85+ Specialty Grade: Every coffee lot evaluated at our roasting lab must pass a strict sensory panel, exhibiting distinct flavor markers and zero primary defects.
  • ★
    Micro-Lot Traceability: We trace every single extraction down to the specific washing station, processing date, and plant variety (SL-28, SL-34, or Ruiru 11).
  • ★
    Precision Roasting: Utilizing software-guided logging system parameters, we roast weekly in small batches to highlight the underlying terroir rather than masking it with char.
