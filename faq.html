Operational FAQ

Clear, descriptive information regarding our cyber-physical space, reservation standards, and sourcing guidelines.

Project Nexus is a conceptual hybrid cyber-physical space combining a premium specialty coffee slow-bar with an optimized, quiet co-working environment. We bridge the gap between third-wave coffee culture and technical productivity hubs.

We source our coffee directly from micro-lot farms across East Africa. Our primary selections highlight washed process lots from Nyeri County, Kenya, and complex, floral honey-processed variants from the Guji region in Ethiopia.

Walk-ins are always welcome for general café seating. However, for our dedicated high-speed workshop desks equipped with individual power arrays, we highly recommend booking a slot in advance using our interactive online reservation form.
